I believe that the only thing that separates us as intelligent beings is our appreciation for aesthetics. It surrounds us — from the symmetry of buildings to the golden ratios in nature. These elements move us and shape meaning. This site explores how design affects the human mind.

Design Techniques

Golden Ratio

Creates harmony because the brain recognizes natural proportions.

Rule of Thirds

Guides attention and creates balanced compositions.

Triangular Composition

Creates stability and keeps the eye moving.

Contrast

Differences in light or size create focus and emotional intensity.
