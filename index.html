Color in Context

Color is not just theory — it directly shapes how we feel in real situations. Below are examples of how color combinations influence emotion and perception.

Horror Scene

Colors: #1B1B2F + #E63946

Dark tones reduce visibility, creating uncertainty. The red introduces danger and tension, triggering fear responses in the brain.

Relaxing Landscape

Colors: #457B9D + #A8DADC

Analogous cool tones create harmony. Blue tones slow heart rate and reduce stress, producing calm and stability.

Playful Design

Colors: #FFBE0B + #3A86FF + #E63946

Triadic colors create strong contrast while staying balanced. This combination stimulates the brain, creating excitement and playfulness.
